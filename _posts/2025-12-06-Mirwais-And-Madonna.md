### In August 2000, these two tracks were released...

<p><a href="https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLe6SA5Qd_oRP7-gK5dXsIyMtCxBg15V8S">Mirwais & Madonna - August 2000 Video Playlist</a></p>

<p>Madonna's vocals are electronically manipulated on the track, with the lyrics having political and social undertones</p>

<p>The <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GYTYXBs6Yhc&list=PLe6SA5Qd_oRP7-gK5dXsIyMtCxBg15V8S&index=1">music video</a> debuted on MTV and VH1 on August 2, 2000, seven months after <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q_LPJllaogU">Cartoon Heroes - Aqua</a> came out.</p>

<p>"When we did the 'Music' video, it was a weird time. [Madonna] was pregnant and we didn't want her to look pregnant—so we had to work around that. We had an idea to do a fun party video with her and her girls, make it a little bling and cowboy hats and all that. We wanted to have some comedy in there and I wanted to do some animation." - Jonas Åkerlund, "GQ", December 2010</a></p>

<p>The production quality of the main-version of "Music" is very different to that of the <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iHfi55FIXPA">offical remix of Music from HQ2</a> ( a musical duo comprising producers/remixers Hex Hector and Mac Quayle )</p>
