### "MADONNA EXCLUSIVE: The Side That NOBODY Knows…"

<p>I've been watching talk show interviews of Madonna for decades, and there seems to be something unusual about this recent interview by Jay Shetty, her first for 9 years it is claimed.</p>

<p>I first thought something was wrong when I heard the audio only as someone else was watching the interview, and thought what was being said was phrased in ways that appeared out of character.</p>

<p>When I watched the video myself, I thought that Madonna didn't look like herself, either physically, or in terms of her body language and mannerisms, and I've formed the opinion that this video could be of a DeepFaked interview.</p>

<p>I'm open to the idea that I'm mistaken, but feel strongly that if such a situation were to occur, that this is a slippery slope and an abuse of technology, and that this should not be able to go unchecked.</p>
